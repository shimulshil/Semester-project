Mental Shower

Welcome to mental Shower Help

The purpose of the app is to keep you comfortable in the classroom. You need to feel free to ask your classmates to change temperature, airflow, humidity or refresh the air in the classroom.

You need to use the poll! Ask your mates is easy: yes or not, not conflicts involve.

Select the changes you want, send a request, and the system will deliver the best option in your zone.

We will send you notifications once in a while to check if you are ok with the climate, and we will send you some advice about when and how to take care of your learning environment.
